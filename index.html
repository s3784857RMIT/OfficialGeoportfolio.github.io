Environmental Systems Analysis

In my Environmental Systems Analysis class at RMIT University, I delved into the intricate world of GIS, aiming to bridge the gap between urban development and ecological preservation.

The challenge was to propose land use management strategies through an academic poster. Urbanisation, with sprawling roads and buildings, often fractures habitats critical for biodiversity. Using GIS, I identified key areas for ecological connectivity, proposing innovative solutions. Land bridges and carefully designed tunnels emerged as practical interventions, tailored to the needs of diverse fauna. Additionally, my research emphasised the vital role of collaboration between government bodies and private landowners.

Through pretend education initiatives and strategic partnerships, I advocated for the preservation of cultural sites and wildlife habitats. This GIS-driven approach not only highlighted the problem but also presented viable, implementable solutions, underscoring the importance of a harmonious balance between human development and nature in our ever-expanding cities.

Spatial / Geographical Information Science Analytics

In my Spatial/Geographical Information Science Analytics class, I undertook a pivotal assignment as a Geospatial Analyst for the Cardinia Shire Council. Tasked with assessing the accessibility of schools in Pakenham, a fast-growing Victoria suburb, my objective was to ensure an equitable distribution of schools relative to residential areas. Employing GIS tools, I meticulously analysed road networks, mesh block data, and school locations, meticulously calculating walking distances. The analysis revealed three prominent routes falling outside the acceptable 40-minute walking radius, indicating gaps in educational accessibility

Utilising ArcMap, I implemented closest-facility analysis, categorising routes based on walking times. Despite challenges with outdated data, I successfully proposed two strategic school locations bridging accessibility gaps, facilitating better education access for the community. This project not only honed my GIS skills but also showcased my ability to address real-world challenges using spatial analysis. Through this assignment, I learned the practical application of geospatial data in enhancing social infrastructure, a valuable lesson I carry forward into my geospatial career endeavors.
